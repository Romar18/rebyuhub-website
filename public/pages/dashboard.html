About RebyuHub and Its Developer

About RebyuHub

RebyuHub is a web platform designed to help students organize their study materials and improve learning through shared reviewers and interactive quizzes. Users can upload and browse reviewer files, categorize study resources, create quizzes, and test their knowledge in an organized and accessible environment.

This project was made possible with the assistance of artificial intelligence tools that supported development, design improvements, and problem solving throughout the creation of the system.
